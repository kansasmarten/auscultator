    COVID-19 Dashboard and Resources

    Looking to talk about COVID-19 with your loved ones, but not sure where to start? We have developed resources and a conversation guide to help you.

    COVID-19 at TMH

    Note: Due to the decreased number of COVID-19 admissions, we will no longer be reporting the total number of COVID-related inpatient cases at TMH starting December 15, 2021. If we see a dramatic increase in admissions, we will reinstate this dashboard to ensure consistent communication and transparency with our community.
